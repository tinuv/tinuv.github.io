# 新句
the wisdom of the ancient Greek **philosophers** is **timeless**

古希腊**哲学家**的智慧是**永恒**的

---
it is as **relevant** today as when it was first written many centuries ago.

正如很多个世纪以前它被第一次写出来,如今依然适用

---
there is no **expiration** date on wisdom

there is no **shelf life** on intelligence 

智慧无保质期

---
developed their 10 golden rules by **turning to(转向)** the men behind that philosophy-Aristole,Socrates,Epictetus and Pythagoras.

通过研究哲学背后的哪些人-亚力士多德,苏格拉底,埃比克泰德和毕达哥拉斯等提出了自己的10条黄金法则

---
the Greeks are always conserned about boxing themselves in,**in terms of(就...而言)** **convictions(信念,确信,定罪)**

希腊人总是担心在信念上困住自己
