# 新句
---
You may have heard some of the fashion industry **horror** stories.

你可能听说过一些时尚业**恐怖**的故事

---
models eating **tissues** or **cotton balls** to **hold off** hunger, and models **collapsing(倒下)** from **hunger-induced** heart attacks just seconds after they **step off** the *runway(跑道,这里为T台)*

模特吃下**纸巾**或者**棉花球**来**抵抗**饥饿,模特们刚**走下T台**的台阶就因为**饥饿诱导**的心脏病而**倒下**

---
**Excessively** **skinny** models have been a point of **controversy** for decades

几十年以来**过分枯瘦**的模特成为**争论**的焦点

---
two researchers say a model’s body mass should be a workspace health and safety issue.

两名研究人员说模特的身体重量应该成为职场健康和安全议题

---
Katherine Record and Bryn Austin made their case for government **regulation** of the fashion industry.

Katherine Record和Bryn Austin提出了政府对时尚产业管理的理由

---
low enough to **indicate** starvation by the World Health Organization’s standard

低到达到了世界卫生组织的标准的饥饿水平

---

**厌食症**比其他任何**心理**疾病造成更多死亡
