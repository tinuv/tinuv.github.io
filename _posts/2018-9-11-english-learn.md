# 新句新词

---
the number of people **hired**--not to teach but to hold meetings--has increased **significantly**

一定数量的人们**雇佣**了,不是去教学的而是去举行会议的人**值得注目地**增加了

---
Recently I attended several meetings where we talked about ways to **retain** students and keep younger **faculty** members from going elsewhere

最近我参加了几场讨论**留住**学生和让年轻的**学院**职工不再流失的会议

---
Every new problem creates a new job for an **administrative** fixer

每一个新的问题都会创造一个**行政**来修复的岗位

---
It's an administrative *sham(欺诈)* of the kind that has *multiplied(使...相乘)* over the last 30 years

这是行政欺诈的一种并且在过去的30年中愈演愈烈

---
Teachers are not people who are great at and *consumed(消耗)* by research and *happen to(碰巧出现)* appear in a classroom

教师不应该是擅长科研且被科研所累而偶尔出现在教师的人

---
Good teaching and research are not **exclusive**, but they are also not **automatic(自动的,天生的) companions**

好的教学与研究并不**排斥**,但也不是**天生的伙伴**
