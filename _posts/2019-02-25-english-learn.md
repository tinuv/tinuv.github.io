# 生词

---
think tank

智库

---

**Sharp** quotes,**intriguing [ɪnˈtri:gɪŋ]** facts and bold new policy **proposals  [prə'poʊzlz]** are **attributed to** the **mysteriours tanks**

尖锐的引用奇妙的事实和加粗的政策建议都归功于神秘的智库

---

What exactly are these **outfits**,which **churn [tʃɜ:rn] out** reports on everything from **Brexit**  to **badgers**

这些到底是什么**组织**,大量生产从英国脱欧到狡诈勒索的报告.

---

Many of Ameracan’s most **venerable [ˈvɛnərəbəl]** tanks

大多数令美国人尊重的智库

---

But think-tanks really **blossomed (开花)** in the second half of the 20th century.

但智库真正爆发在 20 世纪的下半叶.

---

Researchers at the University of Pennsylvania **reckon**** there are now 6,846 of them worldwide

宾夕法尼亚大学的研究人员估计在世界范围内有 6,846 家智库.
