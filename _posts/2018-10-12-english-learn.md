新句

---
as Artificial Intelligence(AI) becomes increasingly **sophisticated**

随着人工智能变得愈加**复杂精致**.

---
if we **figure out** how to turn human values into a programmable code

如果我们**解决**怎样将人类的价值变成可编程的代码

---
as rebots take on more complicated tasks

随着机器人愈加复杂任务

---
if a robot does **chores** around the house

如果机器人确实在房子周围做着**零活**

---
you wouldn't want it to put the pet cat in the oven to make dinner for the hungry children

你肯定不想让它把宠物猫放进微波炉做成晚餐给饥饿的孩子吃.

---
you wouldn't think that's the kind of thing a **properly brought-up(正确抚养长大的,有教养的)** person would to.

你不会认为那是有教养的人会做的事

---
robots could also learn values from drawing patterns from large sets of data on human behavior

机器人也会从大量的关于人类行为模式的数据中汲取信息,学习价值观念.
